Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This page outlines what we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Information you submit voluntarily (e.g., messages via contact).

How we use information

  • To provide and improve the site and content.
  • To respond to inquiries and support requests.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, then delete or anonymize it.

Third parties

We avoid unnecessary tracking. If third-party services are embedded, they may collect their own data per their policies.
